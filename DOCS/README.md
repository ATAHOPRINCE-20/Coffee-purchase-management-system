# Coffee Purchase Management System

## Vision & Value Proposition

The coffee supply chain is often fragmented, with smallholder farmers, local agents, and centralized processors struggling with transparency, tracking, and efficient financial settlements. The **Coffee Purchase Management System** is a unified, cloud-native platform designed to bring professional-grade management to the global coffee trade.

### Why this system is needed
- **Transparency**: Real-time tracking of coffee purchases, advances, and payments ensures fair treatment for farmers and agents.
- **Financial Control**: Managing "Capital Flow" and "Settlements" in a hierarchical structure prevents loss and ensures accountability.
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Integrated **EUDR (European Union Deforestation Regulation)** tracking prepares the supply chain for modern international standards.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Comprehensive reporting on seasons, expenses, and sales allows for better business planning.

### What we are building
A multi-tenant, cloud-scale application that manages the end-to-end coffee procurement process:
- **Hierarchical User Management**: Super Admins (System Owners), Admins (Company Owners), Agents (Field Operators), and Farmers.
- **Purchase & Inventory**: Dynamic price setting per coffee type (Grade A, B, C, Parchment, Cherry) and record-keeping with digital receipts.
- **Financial Instruments**: Management of advance payments (Issue Capital) and final settlements (Clear Balances).
- **Compliance Tracking**: Farmer geolocation and deforestation-free verification (EUDR).

### Core Goals
1.  **Reliability**: Critical financial data must be accurately recorded and immutable.
2.  **Scalability**: Architecture designed for **100X Scale** to handle thousands of entities and millions of transactions.
3.  **Efficiency**: Streamlined field operations for agents using a responsive, low-latency interface.
